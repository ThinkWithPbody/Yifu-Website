About Me

I'm a third year Drawing&Painting student from OCAD University. I have no experience in working with html, css and javascript before. Computer programming seems to be a very flexible tool that we can use to create many interesting stuff which could be done manually. I’m not very good with math so programming is a bit changeling to me. I’m going to try my best and see what I could accomplish with programming and maybe also expand my field of art practice with it.

The images in the middle are some of my recent works. I’ve been working on mixed-media painting for years and most of them are inspired by nature. I prefer to express myself through paintings rougher than words. Paintings are where I could gather energy from and heal myself.
